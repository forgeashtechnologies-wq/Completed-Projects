Our Story

Welcome to Jay Mahal, a premier wedding and event venue in Thoothukudi, Tamil Nadu. Established in 2018, Jay Mahal is an elegant and spacious event space designed to make your special occasions truly memorable.

With over two decades of experience in running esteemed educational institutions in Salem, we ventured into the hospitality sector, bringing our commitment to excellence to the world of celebrations. Our beautifully designed venue spans 21,750 sq. ft., featuring a grand banquet hall, a dedicated dining area, and ample parking space for 50 cars and 100 bikes.

Whether you're planning an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, Jay Mahal is one of the finest venues in Semmarikulam and its surroundings, offering the perfect blend of tradition, elegance, and modern amenities.
